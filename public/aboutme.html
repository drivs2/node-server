A San Juan native and music enthusiast, Daniel majored in Music Business at Northeastern University in Boston. Following his studies, he worked in Los Angeles in the television and film industry. It was in LA that he developed an interest in programming and startups. He drove for both Uber and Lyft and witnessed firsthand how disruptive technology was going to make bring on massive changes in day-to-day life. Upon returning to his native Puerto Rico he decided to apply to Codetrotters in order to learn how to build websites and to gain access into the tech industry. In his spare time he enjoys producing music, the outdoors, the beach, and cycling.
